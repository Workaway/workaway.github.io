Overview

We understand the importance of work-life balance and the benefits of changing environments for productivity and inspiration. This and the flexible work options available today is leading to work from vacation (work-cation) culture among the new age nomads

We are a Silicon Valley startup whose goal is to benefit from this growing market by providing great workcation experiences to our customers

Our Mission

Our mission at Workaway is to provide the ultimate workcation experience for business professionals seeking a healthy work-life balance. We believe that work should not only be productive but also enjoyable, and we strive to create an environment that fosters both.

With carefully selected destinations, work-friendly accommodations, and a wide range of leisure activities, we offer a unique workcation experience that allows our clients to be productive while still enjoying the benefits of a vacation. We are committed to providing personalized service, exceptional amenities, and an unmatched level of professionalism to ensure that our clients have a successful workcation every time they book with us.
