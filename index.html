What is a workcation?

A workcation is a combination of work and vacation, where business professionals can work remotely from a vacation destination. Workcations allow professionals to enjoy the benefits of a vacation while still being able to work and stay productive

Can I bring my family on a workcation?

Yes! Many of our workcation packages are designed to accommodate families, with a range of leisure activities that both adults and children can enjoy. We can also provide child-care services and other family-friendly amenities to ensure that everyone has a great time.

What kind of accommodations do you offer?

We offer a wide range of accommodations, including apartments, condos, and houses that are specifically designed for workcations. Our accommodations feature comfortable workspaces with high-speed internet, spacious desks, and comfortable seating. We also provide fully equipped kitchens, laundry facilities, and other amenities to ensure a comfortable stay.

Can I work while on vacation without any interruptions?

Yes, we strive to provide an environment that is conducive to work, with minimal distractions. However, we also understand that leisure activities are an important part of a workcation, and we can help you balance work and play to ensure that you have a productive yet enjoyable experience.

How do I book a workcation with your company?

Booking a workcation with us is easy. You can contact us through our website, email, or phone, and our team will guide you through the booking process. We'll work closely with you to understand your needs and preferences and tailor our services accordingly.

How do you ensure the safety of your clients during the pandemic?

We take the safety of our clients very seriously and have implemented several measures to ensure their safety during the pandemic. Our accommodations are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before and after each stay, and we follow all local health and safety guidelines. We also offer virtual check-ins and contactless service to minimize contact between our staff and clients.
